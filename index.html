Sambo is a combat sport and essentially a mixed martial art, as it utilizes techniques from other classical martial arts such as boxing, judo, jujutsu, Greco-Roman wrestling, and freestyle wrestling. Sambo is a recognized style of amateur wrestling governed by the UWW in the World Wrestling Championships along with Greco-Roman wrestling and freestyle wrestling. Practiced worldwide, sambo is a martial art with Soviet and Russian origins.

Pros of Combat Sambo training in Toronto

Sambo combines various techniques and is widely accessible. Here are the advantages of the sport:

  1. It is a universal martial art. Regardless of physical fitness level, experience in combat sports, or age, anyone who wishes to can practice it.
  2. Sambo combines the most effective methods and techniques from other combat disciplines. Their effectiveness has been proven in real combat.
  3. A variety of acquired skills and the possibility of combining training with other martial arts classes.
  4. Low risk of injury.
Unique aspects of the sport

Sambo is a mixed system that combines national and international combat techniques. It was created based on Judo and Jiu-Jitsu. Combat Sambo utilizes grappling, strikes, and techniques in both standing and ground positions. This makes it similar to MMA, which is why this discipline is so effective! Kicks, punches, knees, and elbows are all permitted.

  • Combat is conducted at close and medium ranges, with full contact;
  • Holds and submission locks are permitted;
  • Sambo training is focused on protection and self-defense, rather than attack
