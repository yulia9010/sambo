Meet Combat Sambo

Combat Sambo training in Toronto

Combat Sambo is recognized worldwide as one of the most effective martial arts.

Key Features of Sambo

Heritage:
  • A dynamic martial art shaped by the best of judo, jiu-jitsu, boxing, and traditional wrestling styles.
What Sets It Apart:
  • Unlike sport sambo, combat sambo allows strikes and chokeholds—making it a more complete and realistic fighting system, while sport sambo focuses purely on grappling.
Gear:
  • Athletes train and compete in specialized equipment, including a distinctive jacket with grip tabs, shorts, sambo shoes, and protective gear such as helmets and MMA-style gloves.
Purpose:
  • Designed for real-world effectiveness, combat sambo emphasizes rapid control and decisive neutralization of an opponent.


What truly sets it apart is its seamless blend of grappling and striking. Fighters can attack both standing and on the ground, using chokes, joint locks, and powerful throws. This versatility makes combat sambo a highly practical and adaptable fighting system.

It’s no coincidence that combat sambo is widely regarded as a top-tier method of self-defense and is used in the training programs of military and law enforcement units.

Combat Sambo is a homegrown martial art developed in the Soviet Union in the early 20th century. Originally created to train military personnel and special forces, it was designed for maximum effectiveness in real combat situations.
